Who we are looking for

With a focus on academic excellence, leadership potential, and community service, we are committed to nurturing talent and empowering the next generation of change-makers. Join us in our journey to create a more equitable and inclusive society by supporting the dreams of these exceptional students.

High academic acheivers

we are looking at individual's exceptional performance in their academic pursuits. It signifies a level of excellence and mastery in academic endeavors, reflecting a strong commitment to learning, intellectual growth, and the acquisition of knowledge.We understand the hard work, dedication, and perseverance it takes to excel academically, especially for students facing financial challenges. That is why we are here to recognize and support your exceptional achievements.

Possesion of strong leadership qualities

We work with college students who share set of skills, attributes, and behaviors that enable them to effectively guide, inspire, and influence others towards a common goal within a college or university setting and we expect that leadership qualities can be developed and strengthened over time through self-reflection, learning from experiences, seeking feedback,and actively engaging in leadership roles and opportunities.

Extraordinary Extracurricular activities

Extraordinary Extracurricular activities Extracurricular activities are voluntary, non-academic pursuits that students engage in outside of their regular academic curriculum. These activities typically take place within the school or college environment and offer students opportunities to explore their interests, develop new skills, and engage with their peers in a more information and recreational setting.We are looking for students who engaged in sports, performing arts, student led organisation,academic competions, volunteer activities etc
